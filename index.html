USRC

Delivering hands-on experience for the next generation of roboticists.

What we do

USRC serves two primary functions: Supporting USYD Robotics teams in external competitions, hosting USYD based robotics events.

Supporting USYD Robotics teams to facilitate their entering robotics competitions by providing a staging

Supporting our teams

As an individual robotics student, it can be hard to know where to look to find challenging Robotics competitions. So, at USRC, we actively find and organise robotics teams to participate in external competitions.

Additionally, University robotics teams are often temporary organisations, so USRC aims to help retain technical and logistical knowledge between competition teams.

Who we are

USRC was founded in 2017 with a mission to deliver a more hands on robotics experience to USYD students.

Since then, we've grown to over 50 members and run up to 5 robotics teams every year.

Our community

USRC meets on a fortnightly basis, where our members come together to discuss topics in robotics over light refreshments.

We welcome members from all stages and backgrounds, from first-year to postgraduate, and beyond!

Our staff

Our executive team is there to handle logistics and organisation of our hosted events and to help facilitate and train others for competitions and more.
